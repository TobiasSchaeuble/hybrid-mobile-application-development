Ionic Conference is a one-day conference featuring talks from the Ionic team. It is focused on Ionic applications being built with Ionic 2. This includes migrating apps from Ionic 1 to Ionic 2, Angular concepts, Webpack, Sass, and many other technologies used in Ionic 2. Tickets are completely sold out, and we’re expecting more than 1000 developers – making this the largest Ionic conference ever!
